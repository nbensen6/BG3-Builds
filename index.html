❄️ THE ICE SORCERER ❄️

🧊 Draconic Bloodline Sorcerer (White Dragon) 🧊

🌨️ Build Overview

A phenomenal AOE damage dealer with incredible crowd control. Freeze enemies solid, make them slip and miss their turns, or delete them with doubled cold damage. The Ray of Frost machine gun turns you into an economical ice cannon, while Cone of Cold acts as a devastating shotgun blast.

Class Split: 12 Draconic Sorcerer (Pure) — White Dragon Ancestry

💡 CORE MECHANIC: Wet + Cold = Frozen! Frozen enemies skip their entire turn. Cold damage is DOUBLED against wet targets!

❄️ Level Progression

Level 1: Draconic Bloodline (White) — Ray of Frost
Level 2: Metamagic — Twin Spell, Quicken Spell
Level 3: Level 2 spells — Hold Person, Mirror Image
Level 4: Feat: +2 CHA (to 18)
Level 5: Level 3 spells — Haste, Counterspell
Level 6: Elemental Affinity — Add CHA to cold damage!
Level 7: Level 4 spells — Ice Storm
Level 8: Feat: +2 CHA (to 20)
Level 9: Level 5 spells — Cone of Cold, Hold Monster
Level 10: Metamagic — Heightened Spell
Level 11: Level 6 spells — Chain Lightning (scroll)
Level 12: Feat: Alert OR War Caster

🏹 THE STEALTH ARCHER 🏹

🌑 Gloom Stalker / Assassin / Fighter / War Cleric 🎯

🎯 Build Overview

The STRONGEST build for solo Honor Mode. This build has the most powerful early game, dealing 50-100 damage average in Act 1, over 100 in Act 2, and four-digit Nova in Act 3. Assassinate guarantees critical hits on surprised enemies.

Class Split: 5 Gloom Stalker Ranger / 4 Assassin Rogue / 2 Fighter / 1 War Cleric

👑 #1 SOLO BUILD: There is no stronger class for a solo Honor Mode run. Two attacks at level 3. Heavy armor at level 1. You leave the Nautiloid with 17 AC!

🌙 Level Progression

Ranger 1: Heavy Armor proficiency! Leave Nautiloid with 17 AC
Ranger 2: Archery fighting style (+2 to ranged attacks)
Ranger 3: Gloom Stalker — +3 initiative, Dread Ambusher
Ranger 4: Feat: Sharpshooter (+10 damage, -5 to hit)
Ranger 5: Extra Attack, Pass Without Trace
Rogue 1: Sneak Attack (1d6), Expertise
Rogue 2: Cunning Action
Rogue 3: Assassin — Assassinate (auto-crit on surprised!)
Rogue 4: Feat: Alert (+5 initiative)
Fighter 1: Defense (+1 AC)
Fighter 2: Action Surge!
War Cleric 1: Bonus action attack

💀 Assassinate Explained

ASSASSINATE: AMBUSH
Attack rolls against a SURPRISED creature are GUARANTEED CRITICAL HITS.

How to proc Surprise: Go invisible → Attack enemy in their sight radius → That attack CRITS automatically → With proper gear: 200-300+ damage per arrow!

🔥 Nova Round Breakdown

🎯 Maximum Nova (Level 12):

1. Out of combat attack (1 attack)
2. Enter combat → Alacrity restores action/bonus
3. Action: 2 attacks + Dread Ambusher = 3 attacks
4. Bloodlust proc on kill = 1 attack
5. Haste action = 1 attack
6. Action Surge = 2 more attacks
7. War Cleric bonus action attack = 1 attack

TOTAL: 8-10 ATTACKS ROUND ONE!

⚔️ THE BATTLE MASTER ⚔️

🛡️ Pure Fighter — Steel and Discipline 🛡️

💪 Build Overview

The quintessential martial powerhouse. Simple, devastating, and effective from level one. Hand your fighter heavy armor and a big stick, and start hitting things — it's really that simple. Three attacks at level 11 plus Action Surge makes this build capable of one-turning even Raphael.

Class Split: 12 Battle Master Fighter (Pure)

⚔️ SIMPLE POWER: Fighter is strong from level 1 with huge power spikes early. Almost no gear competition. Perfect for any player, especially beginners!

🤜 THE TAVERN BRAWLER 🤜

🔥 Open Hand Monk / Thief Rogue / Fighter 🔥

💥 Build Overview

Massive single-target damage with insane action economy and unbelievable battlefield control. Zero gear requirements — you don't need any equipment to be effective! Ki points refresh on short rest, making this perfect for pushing without long rests.

Class Split: 8 Open Hand Monk / 3 Thief Rogue / 1 Fighter

🔥 KARLACH PICK: Soul Coins add 1d4 fire damage to punches, but they're BUGGED and actually do 2d4! Works from Act 1!

✊ Level Progression

Monk 1: Martial Arts, Unarmored Defense
Monk 2: Ki Points, Flurry of Blows
Monk 3: Open Hand — Flurry adds prone/push!
Monk 4: Feat: Tavern Brawler (STR mod x2!)
Monk 5: Stunning Strike, Extra Attack
Monk 6: Ki-Empowered Strikes, Manifestations
Rogue 1: Sneak Attack, Expertise
Rogue 2: Cunning Action
Rogue 3: Thief — EXTRA BONUS ACTION!
Monk 7-8: Evasion, +2 DEX or WIS
Fighter 1: Action Surge

🗡️ Fighter Build - Patch 8 💙

A Lightning-focused Eldritch Knight Fighter combining martial prowess with arcane spellcasting. Patch 8's reworked War Magic allows Booming Blade to trigger a bonus action attack for up to four attacks per turn.

Class: 12 Eldritch Knight Fighter (Pure)

⚡ WAR MAGIC (PATCH 8): After casting a cantrip, make a bonus action weapon attack! Booming Blade counts = 3 attacks + 1 bonus = 4 attacks per turn!

📊 Quick Reference

Class: 12 Eldritch Knight Fighter (Pure)
Primary Stats: STR 16+, DEX 14, CON 14
Key Features: War Magic, Eldritch Strike, Extra Attack (2)
Attacks per Turn: 4 (War Magic), 7 (Action Surge)
