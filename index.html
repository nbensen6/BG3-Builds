The Ice Sorcerer

Draconic Bloodline Sorcerer (White Dragon)

Build Overview

A phenomenal AOE damage dealer with incredible crowd control. Freeze enemies solid, make them slip and miss their turns, or delete them with doubled cold damage. The Ray of Frost machine gun turns you into an economical ice cannon, while Cone of Cold acts as a devastating shotgun blast.

Class: 12 Draconic Sorcerer (Pure) - White Dragon Ancestry

CORE MECHANIC: Wet + Cold = Frozen! Frozen enemies skip their entire turn. Apply Wet with Create Water or water bottles, then blast with cold spells. Cold damage is DOUBLED against wet targets!

Level Progression

Level 1: Draconic Bloodline (White) - Ray of Frost, one other cantrip Level 2: Metamagic - Twin Spell, Quicken Spell Level 3: Level 2 spells - Hold Person, Mirror Image Level 4: Feat: +2 CHA (to 18) Level 5: Level 3 spells - Haste, Counterspell Level 6: Elemental Affinity - Add CHA to cold damage! Level 7: Level 4 spells - Ice Storm Level 8: Feat: +2 CHA (to 20) Level 9: Level 5 spells - Cone of Cold, Hold Monster Level 10: Metamagic - Heightened Spell; Ray of Frost = 3d8 Level 11: Level 6 spells - Chain Lightning (scroll) Level 12: Feat: Alert OR War Caster

The Stealth Archer

Gloom Stalker / Assassin / Fighter / War Cleric

Build Overview

The STRONGEST build for solo Honor Mode. This build has the most powerful early game, dealing 50-100 damage average in Act 1, over 100 in Act 2, and four-digit Nova in Act 3. Assassinate guarantees critical hits on surprised enemies.

Class Split: 5 Gloom Stalker / 4 Assassin / 2 Fighter / 1 War Cleric

#1 SOLO BUILD: There is no stronger class for a solo Honor Mode run. Two attacks at level 3. Heavy armor at level 1. You leave the Nautiloid with 17 AC!

Level Progression

Ranger 1: Heavy Armor proficiency! Leave Nautiloid with 17 AC Ranger 2: Archery fighting style (+2 to ranged attacks) Ranger 3: Gloom Stalker - +3 initiative, Dread Ambusher (extra attack + 1d8) Ranger 4: Feat: Sharpshooter (+10 damage, -5 to hit) Ranger 5: Extra Attack, Pass Without Trace (+10 stealth!) Rogue 1: Sneak Attack (1d6), Expertise Rogue 2: Cunning Action Rogue 3: Assassin - Assassinate (auto-crit on surprised!) Rogue 4: Feat: Alert (+5 initiative) Fighter 1: Defense (+1 AC) Fighter 2: Action Surge! War Cleric 1: Bonus action attack

Assassinate Explained

ASSASSINATE: AMBUSH
Attack rolls against a SURPRISED creature are GUARANTEED CRITICAL HITS.

How to proc: Go invisible > Attack enemy in sight radius > That attack CRITS automatically > With gear: 200-300+ damage per arrow!

Nova Round Breakdown

Maximum Nova (Level 12): 1. Out of combat attack (1 attack) 2. Enter combat - Alacrity restores action/bonus 3. Action: 2 attacks + Dread Ambusher = 3 attacks 4. Bloodlust proc on kill = 1 attack 5. Haste action = 1 attack 6. Action Surge = 2 more attacks 7. War Cleric bonus action = 1 attack TOTAL: 8-10 ATTACKS ROUND ONE!
